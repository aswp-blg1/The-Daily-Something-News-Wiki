# Devs: AI Upskilling Program Launches in ECI

Developers in East Central Indiana, prepare for a significant upgrade! Innovation Connector and LaunchReady.ai have forged a partnership to deliver cutting-edge strategic AI learning programs directly to our region. This initiative offers a focused curriculum on practical AI implementation, covering core machine learning fundamentals, data analysis, and advanced model deployment techniques. The primary objective is to empower and upskill the local developer community, enabling seamless integration of AI capabilities into existing projects and fostering new, AI-driven innovations. Participants will gain access to hands-on training, valuable resources, and opportunities to build impactful real-world AI applications. This is a direct pathway to expanding your technical toolkit and driving regional tech advancement.
