# Dev Alert: Claude Fable 5 Banned at Microsoft

Developers at Microsoft are now prohibited from using Claude Fable 5, a significant policy shift driven by concerns over potential sensitive data leaks. This restriction highlights the growing challenge for developers in securely integrating AI tools into their workflows without compromising proprietary information or project specifics. The incident serves as a stark reminder to thoroughly vet AI services for their data handling policies, especially when feeding them internal code or confidential documentation. Developers must prioritize secure AI practices, potentially through sandboxed environments or internal-only models, to mitigate risks of intellectual property exposure.
