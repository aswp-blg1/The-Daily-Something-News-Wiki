# ECI AI Learning Alliance: Dev Insights

Developers in East Central Indiana can now access enhanced AI learning opportunities through a new partnership between Innovation Connector and LaunchReady.ai. This alliance aims to deliver practical, skill-focused AI training relevant to current industry demands. The curriculum will cover core AI concepts, machine learning fundamentals, and practical application development, designed to upskill local talent. Developers will gain insights into deploying AI solutions and contributing to innovative projects, fostering regional technological advancement. This initiative is a strategic step to cultivate a stronger AI-savvy workforce, ready to tackle complex challenges and drive innovation locally. Read more about the region's AI readiness: https://thedailysomethingnews.com/east-central-indiana-gears-up-for-ai-revolution-innovation-connector-and-launchready-ai-forge-strategic-learning-alliance/.
