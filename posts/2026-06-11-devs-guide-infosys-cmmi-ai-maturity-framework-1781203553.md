# Devs Guide: Infosys & CMMI AI Maturity Framework

Infosys and the CMMI Institute are co-creating an Enterprise AI Maturity Framework, providing a structured approach for integrating AI into enterprise systems. This framework offers developers clear guidelines for evaluating, implementing, and optimizing AI applications. It helps identify key architectural considerations, data governance requirements, and deployment strategies necessary for scaling AI effectively. For development teams, understanding these maturity levels will streamline project planning and execution, ensuring AI solutions meet enterprise-grade standards and performance. This initiative promises to be a valuable resource for engineers working on complex AI deployments. For more details on this collaboration, read the full report here: https://thedailysomethingnews.com/infosys-and-cmmi-institute-forge-path-for-enterprise-ai-excellence-with-groundbreaking-maturity-framework/.
