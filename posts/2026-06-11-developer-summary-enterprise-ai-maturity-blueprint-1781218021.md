# Developer Summary: Enterprise AI Maturity Blueprint

Infosys has partnered with the CMMI Institute to architect a foundational Enterprise AI Maturity Framework. This initiative is critical for developers implementing AI solutions, offering a structured pathway to ensure ethical, scalable, and effective deployments. The framework will define key stages of AI adoption, from initial experimentation to advanced operationalization, providing guidance on data governance, model validation, and responsible development practices. For engineers, this translates to clearer benchmarks and standardized approaches for building robust AI systems. Infosys's early involvement has garnered significant recognition, highlighting their commitment to shaping the future of enterprise AI development.
