# U of U Rolls Out First AI Bachelor's Degree

The University of Utah is introducing the state's first Bachelor of Science degree in Artificial Intelligence, extending its foundational AI instruction which dates back to 1973. This program offers developers a comprehensive pathway to specialize in AI, covering key areas like machine learning, deep learning, natural language processing, and computer vision. The curriculum is structured to equip graduates with practical coding skills, algorithm design, and system integration capabilities essential for building intelligent applications. Students will gain hands-on experience with popular AI frameworks and tools, preparing them for roles in software development, data science, and AI engineering.
