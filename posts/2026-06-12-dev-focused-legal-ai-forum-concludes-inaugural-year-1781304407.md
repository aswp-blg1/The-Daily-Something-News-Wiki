# Dev-Focused Legal AI Forum Concludes Inaugural Year

The GW Law AI Forum has wrapped its inaugural year, providing a crucial platform for understanding AI's practical implications in legal development. Discussions centered on leveraging AI for automated legal research, document generation, and predictive analytics, highlighting the tooling and methodologies developers might encounter. The forum explored the intersection of legal frameworks with AI/ML model deployment, focusing on ethical AI development practices and data security challenges pertinent to legal data. It underscored the need for robust APIs and SDKs that comply with evolving regulatory landscapes, preparing future legal professionals to collaborate effectively with engineering teams on RegTech solutions.
