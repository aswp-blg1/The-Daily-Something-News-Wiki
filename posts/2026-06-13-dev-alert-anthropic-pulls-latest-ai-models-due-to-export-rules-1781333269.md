# Dev Alert: Anthropic Pulls Latest AI Models Due to Export Rules

Developers relying on Anthropic's bleeding-edge AI models should note their recent announcement: the latest iterations have been taken offline to comply with new export controls. It's a stark reminder that even cloud-based AI services are subject to national and international trade policies.
