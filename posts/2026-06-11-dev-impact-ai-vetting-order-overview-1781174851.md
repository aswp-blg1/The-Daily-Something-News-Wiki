# Dev Impact: AI Vetting Order Overview

Developers building AI systems now face new considerations following Trump's executive order mandating rigorous vetting. This means a heightened focus on model transparency, bias detection, and robust security testing throughout the development pipeline. Prepare to integrate more comprehensive validation stages, documenting decision-making processes and potential vulnerabilities. The order will likely drive demand for tools facilitating auditable AI and explainable models. It's an important shift, pushing us to bake in safety and accountability from the start, rather than as an afterthought.
